## Description

**Create unique pixel art designs and wear them on custom t-shirts!**

Pixel Tee is the easiest way to design retro-style pixel art and order high-quality printed t-shirts delivered straight to your door.

**SIMPLE DESIGN TOOLS**
• Draw on a 20x20 pixel canvas - perfect for creating iconic, nostalgic designs
• 11 vibrant colors to bring your creations to life
• Brush and eraser tools for precise control
• Unlimited undo/redo - experiment freely

**INSTANT PREVIEW**
• See your design on a realistic t-shirt mockup
• Choose from 5 shirt colors: White, Black, Navy, Charcoal, Red
• Visualize exactly what you'll receive

**EASY ORDERING**
• Simple checkout with secure Stripe payment
• Free worldwide shipping included
• High-quality print on premium cotton shirts
• Ships in 5-10 business days

**PERFECT FOR**
• Gamers who love retro pixel aesthetics
• Artists exploring minimalist design
• Unique personalized gifts
• Custom merch for your brand or channel

No design experience needed. Just tap, draw, and order. Your pixel art masterpiece arrives at your door ready to wear.

## Review Notes for Apple
This app allows users to create simple pixel art designs and order custom-printed t-shirts.

Payment processing: We use Stripe for secure payments. Physical goods (t-shirts) are exempt from Apple's in-app purchase requirements per App Store Review Guideline 3.1.3(e).

Fulfillment: Orders are fulfilled through Teemill, a print-on-demand service. Shirts are printed and shipped directly to customers.

Test account: Not required - app works without login.
